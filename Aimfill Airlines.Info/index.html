Bharathiyar university
state university ,
Coimbatore india

The Bharathiar University was established at Coimbatore by the Government of Tamilnadu in February, under the provision of the Bharathiar University Act, 1981 (Act 1 of 1982). The Postgraduate Centre of the University of Madras, which was functioning in Coimbatore before 1982 formed the core of the Bharathiar University. In May, 1985 the University received the recognition from University Grants Commission (UGC) Delhi for the purpose of grants. The University is “A” Grade university affiliated by NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council). It has been ranked No. 2 in all India University rankings.
